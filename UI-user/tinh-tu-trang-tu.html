Tính từ & Trạng từ

Danh sách các cặp tính từ – trạng từ thường gặp trong giao tiếp.

Tính từ (Adjective) Trạng từ (Adverb) Ví dụ
quick quickly He is quick. / He runs quickly.
happy happily She is happy. / She speaks happily.
careful carefully Be careful. / Drive carefully.
good well She is good at math. / She speaks English well.
fast fast A fast car. / He drives fast.
